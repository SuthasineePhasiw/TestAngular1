Write a program that determine whether or not an integer input is a leap year. Definition of leap year:
Rule 1: A year is called leap year if it is divisible by 400. Example: 1600, 2000 etc. are leap year while 1500, 1700 are not leap year.
Rule 2: If year is not divisible by 400 as well as 100 but it is divisible by 4 then that year are also leap year. Example: 2004, 2008, 1012 are leap year.
